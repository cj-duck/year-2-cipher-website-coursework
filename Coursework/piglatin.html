Pig Latin (Igpay Atinlay)

Pig Latin is a cipher in which words are altered to be unrecognisable to those who do not know the rules. In Pig Latin:

  • Words that begin with consonant sounds, have all the letters before the initial vowel placed at the end of the word sequence and then "ay" is added to the end.
  • Words that begin with vowel sounds just have "way" "ay" or "yay" added to the end ("yay" is used on this page)
